## coachType Rules
- **daily** — something to do or confirm every day (habit)
- **weekly** — something to do or advance every week
- **yearly** — something that recurs every year (annual trip, annual checkup etc.)
- **budget** — a purchase, tracked against monthly budget
- **none** — one-time task, dream, or blocked item with no time frame

---

## Priority Rule of Thumb
- **Higher priority** = task affects someone else / linked to another person waiting on you
- **Lower priority** = task depends only on yourself, no one else is affected

## The Core Philosophy (Matar's Words)

> "I tried to set a hard schedule but it's simply not how life is."

The app is not a rigid planner. It's a compass — always has a clear answer to "what should I be doing right now?" so instead of doom scrolling, you open the app and it points you toward the next small step toward your goals. Life fluctuates, but the direction stays constant.

Hard schedules failed. This system works *with* the fluctuation, not against it.
